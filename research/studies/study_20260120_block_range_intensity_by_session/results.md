1. **No Predictive Claim:** These results describe historical associations. They do NOT predict future outcomes.

3. **No Session Ranking:** Results do NOT identify any session as "better" or "worse" for trading. Differences are descriptive, not prescriptive.

5. **No Optimization:** Session boundaries (6-hour UTC bins) are fixed. No fitting or optimization was performed to maximize any metric.

6. **Non-Canonical:** This study is a downstream research artifact. Results do NOT modify canonical Option B/C definitions.
